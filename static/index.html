Our Founders

Umesh, CEO - Bio

A senior executive with over 25+ years of experience, Umesh has helped develop the vision, strategy and execution plan for several innovative technology products. Most recently, Umesh was the VP/GM for a $2B+ Data Center switching business at Cisco leading engineering, product management and operations for Nexus 7000 and MDS switching products and the NXOS operating system. Umesh has a Masters from Duke and a Bachelors from IIT Delhi and has over 26 patents to date.

Murali, VP Eng - Bio

With 20+ years of expertise in Distributed Systems, Networking, and Storage, Murali was most recently the VP of Software Engineering for the Data Center group at Cisco, having joined Cisco through the acquisition of Andiamo Networks. Prior to Cisco, Murali was Chief Architect at Shasta Networks (acquired by Nortel), where he led the architecture for the innovative Shasta Broadband IP Services Platform, a distributed multi-subscriber services engine. Murali has a Masters in CS from Arizona State University, a certificate from the Stanford Executive Program and over 20 patents to date.

Ranga, CTO - Bio

Ranga has architected and developed several high performance distributed operating systems, networking, and storage data center products over the last 15 years. Most recently, he was Sr. Director at Cisco's Data Center business unit, which he joined through the acquisition of Andiamo Networks where he was one of the lead architects for SANOS operating system. Ranga started his career at SGI as an IRIX kernel engineer for the Origin series of ccNUMA servers. Ranga has a Masters in EE from Stanford University and several patents in networking and storage.
